About the company

Ukrainian company JV CITICOM, LTD has been in the market since 1993 and specialises in the development, production and assembly of PCB using THT and SMT technology. During the twenty years in which we have been active we have become the leading company in the aforementioned field in the Czech Republic. However we are also in the process of penetrating the markets of other EU states and have in recent years gained experience in the Canadian market.

Our company is dynamic in its development. We now employ over 30 members of staff and are equipped with the most recent technology supplied by leading manufacturers.
